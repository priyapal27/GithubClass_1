Q6: Write a Python Program to find the area of triangle # Three sides of the triangle a, b and c are provided by the user.

#Area of a triangle

import math
a = float(input("Enter first side of triangle: "))
b = float(input("Enter second side of triangle: "))
c = float(input("Enter third side of triangle: "))

s = (a+b+c)/2

area = int(math.sqrt(s*(s-a)*(s-b)*(s-c)))
print(area)

Output:
Enter first side of triangle:  3
Enter second side of triangle:  4
Enter third side of triangle:  6
5
